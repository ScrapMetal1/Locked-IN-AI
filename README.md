# Locked In — AI

**Locked In — AI** is the ultimate Deep Work Logger and Focus Helper, powered by Google Cloud Vertex AI. It intelligently analyzes your browsing to block distractions and gamifies your productivity.

## Features
*   **AI-Powered Analysis:** Uses Gemini 3 Flash to filter content in real-time.
*   **Deep Work Logger:** Automatically tracks your focused hours and productivity streaks.
*   **Leaderboards:** Compete with others to see who can stay "Locked In" the longest.
*   **Activity Dashboards:** Visual analytics of your habits and focus sessions.
*   **Pomodoro Tracker:** built-in focus timers to manage work intervals.
*   **Smart Blocking:** Custom filtering based on your specific goals.
*   **Cloud Architecture:** Scalable performance with Google Cloud Functions.
